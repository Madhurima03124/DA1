What is SDG4?

SDG4 (Sustainable Development Goal 4) aims to "ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all" by 2030. This goal focuses on:

  • Universal primary and secondary education
  • Equal access to technical/vocational and higher education
  • Relevant skills for decent work and employment opportunities
  • Gender equality and inclusion in education
  • Universal youth and adult literacy
  • Increased focus on early childhood education and pre-primary education

These targets aim to address existing inequalities in education and improve access to quality learning opportunities.
